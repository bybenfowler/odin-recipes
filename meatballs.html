Swedish Meatballs

How to make swedish meatballs.

These Swedish meatballs will be the most delicous balls you've ever had. Those Italian balls have nothing on their Nordic cousins.
Try serving the meatballs over noodles or your favorite vegtables for a delicious and easy meal.

Ingredients

  • 80/20 ground beef
  • Ground pork
  • Flat leaf parsley
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Beef stock
  • Lemon
  • Spices: Ground allspice, nutmeg, salt and pepper
  • Heavy cream
